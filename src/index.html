“Pushtisanskardham“ is a project proposed by Pushtisanskar Sansthan (Institution) that will take shape near Junagadh spread out approximately in 35-40 acres. The project will shape up under the able guidance of Rev. Goswami shri Kishorechandraji Maharajshri and Goswami shri Piyushbavashri (MotiHaveli-Junagadh).

Inauguration on 26 November, 2026

Activities

Pushtisanskar Pathshala

Nurturing Young Minds with Eternal Values

At Pushtisanskar Pathshala, children immerse in reciting shloks, singing devotional music, and learning the timeless teachings of Shri Vallabhacharyaji, nurturing purpose, joy, and spiritual growth. With 180+ centres across Gujarat, weekly sessions blend Pushtimarg values with all-round personality development, complementing their regular studies.

Join the Pathshala Know How it Works

Pushtisanskar Vidyapeeth

Deepening Devotion Through Pushtimarg Wisdom

Pushti Sanskar Vidyapeeth helps devotees understand the core tenets of Pushtimarg through the original granthas of Shri Vallabhacharyaji. Starting with Pushtisiddhant Pravesh and advancing to key scriptures, over 4,600 students from 17 countries deepen their devotion and spiritual understanding.

Join the Vidyapeeth Know How it Works

Pushtisanskar Unity

Uniting Youth for the Cause of Dharma

Pushtisanskar Unity brings together young devotees to uphold and spread the values of Pushtimarg. It serves as a vibrant platform where youth engage in spiritual learning, devotional activities, seva, and cultural programs that strengthen their roots in dharma. It nurtures leadership, unity, and purpose, inspiring youth to uphold eternal values and positively impact society.
