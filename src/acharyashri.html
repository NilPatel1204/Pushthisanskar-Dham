Acharyashri

Goswami Shri Kishorechandraji Maharajshri

(Moti Haveli - Junagadh)

Guiding Force

Goswami Shree Kishorechandraji Maharajshri is born in the lineage of Mahaprabhu Shree Vallabhacharyaji. He is a learned acharya; well versed with the spiritual knowledge and practical implications of it. His profound knowledge, experience and blessings are motivational for our mission.

Gauseva is one of the important aspect of our culture. Specially in Shree Krishna Bhakti traditions; Gauseva is considered as an essential part of devotion. In general also; Gausanskruti need to be protected for the preservance of relegion. With this motive, Shree Kishorechandraji Maharajshri has created social awareness about the necessity of Gauseva. With his inspiration; more than 100 Gaushala are established in the saurastra region of Gujarat.

Mahabrabhu Shree Vallabhacharya Gurukul(Choki-Sorath) and Shree Vallabh Gaushala(Vadala) are the institutes established under his guidance. In a way he is the pathmaker, when everyone engaged in Haveli's, Darshans and Manoraths, he stood up in the change in the vaishnava society while combining relegion with education and social reforms.

“We are blessed to have such a divine personality as our guiding force.”

Visionary Executing Force

Adopting this deﬁnition, Our Acharya Goswami Shri Piyush Bavashri constantly trying to fulﬁl his duties as an Acharya; has quite an astounding personality.

He himself is living as per the divine path shown by Shri Vallabh; and always inspiring hisdisciples to walk on the same path. It is his strong belief that the only way to make a life whole and 'anandmay' is to understand the true siddhant show by Shri Vallabh and try our best to follow the same in our lifestyle; and also believes that the best way to inculcate this knowledge is to impart them to children in their early ages.

To implement this vision, he is constantly active through various activities for one single goal; 'Sanskar for Children and Youths'.

And the result of these immense hard work, Pushtisanskar Pathshala for children and Pushtisanskar Vidyapeeth for youth came into existence and ﬂourished under his leadership. Thousands of children and youth are taking the advantage of this centres and getting the knowledge of living life in its true form of spirituality.

“Pushtisanskar Dham is a project envisioned by him to provide profound energy to all this activities and to achieve the greater heights towards education and sanskar for our children and youths.”

Goswami Shri Piyush Bavashri
